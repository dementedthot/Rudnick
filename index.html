About Rudnick MFG

Family-owned manufacturing with serious attention to the finish.

Rudnick MFG is a hands-on machine shop focused on precision CNC work, aluminum fabrication, and long-term customer relationships. The difference is in the details: clean edges, deburred parts, careful finishing, and the kind of practical craftsmanship that only comes from people who care about the work.

Whether the project is a prototype, a small batch, or a repeat production run, the goal stays the same: produce parts that are accurate, clean, and ready to use.
